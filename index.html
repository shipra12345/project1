Fabrics



Textile


A textile is a flexible material that consists of
network of artificial fibers.Yarn is produced by spinning of raw fibers like wool,cotton,hemp etc.Textiles are formed by weaving,knitting etc.

Uses


Textiles have a variety of uses,the most comman one is they are used for clothing and for containers such as bags and baskets.In household they are used in carpenting,towels.

Treatments


Textiles are often dyed,with fabrics that are available in almost every color.The dying process often requires several dozen of gallons of water for each pound of clothing.
